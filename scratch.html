My Better Half

I met the love of my life in a taco truck parking lot in western Kansas. What are the odds of meeting in such a strange place? I thank the Lord for this one because we met as missionaries for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints! Brody had served in Mexico for 15 months before being sent home for COVID. He was then reassigned to the Kansas Wichita Mission, which is where we met! The sparks didn't fly until a few months after we had both been home from our missions. We dated for 3 months before getting engaged, and 2 months after the engagement, we eloped. We have been happily married for over a year now.
